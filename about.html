OUR STORY

Redefining the sports experience with world-class facilities and unmatched passion.

Our Mission

To provide professional-grade sports infrastructure accessible to everyone. We believe that every athlete, amateur or professional, deserves to play on a pitch that brings out their best performance without compromising on safety or luxury.

Our Vision

To become the ultimate hub for sports enthusiasts globally, fostering a community of passionate players, and setting the gold standard for premium sports facilities across the nation.

The TurfX Journey

Born from a passion for the game, TurfX started in 2014 when a group of professional players realized the lack of quality playing surfaces in the city. What began as a single 5-a-side pitch has now evolved into a state-of-the-art multi-sport arena.

Every blade of grass, every floodlight, and every locker room detail has been meticulously chosen to replicate the environment of top-tier international stadiums.
